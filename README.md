## About Cybersecurity
Cybersecurity is the practice of **protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks**.  
Key areas include:

- **Network Security**: Protecting internal and external networks from intrusions.  
- **Application Security**: Securing software from vulnerabilities.  
- **Penetration Testing**: Simulating attacks to find weaknesses.  
- **Cryptography**: Encrypting information to protect privacy and integrity.  
- **Incident Response**: Detecting, analyzing, and responding to cyber threats.  

Cybersecurity is critical in today’s digital world, where attackers can exploit **any vulnerability** to steal data, disrupt services, or compromise systems.
